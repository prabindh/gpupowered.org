Prabindh Sundareson has been working in the areas of High Performance Graphics and Computer vision technologies applied to Consumer products, as it evolved from iPods to iPhones. He is currently a Researcher in Samsung Research, Bangalore. Formerly, he worked as a Member of Group Technical Staff at Texas Instruments in Dallas and Bangalore. A Senior Member of the IEEE, Prabindh is the Founder Chairman of the IEEE Consumer Electronics Society Bangalore chapter, and the author of GPUPowered.Org. He holds 5 USPTO patents, and has several pending at the USPTO. He graduated from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.
